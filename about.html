This website allows searching employers who sponsor H1b visas. The H1b database has LCAs filed for recent years.

Search criteria could be employer name or zipcode of employer location. Additional search criteria includes filter by salary range offered for the position, filter by year of filing, filter by job title of the position and option to exclude H1 dependent employers.

These are the fields that are shown for each LCA: Employer Name, Job Title, Start Date, Salary and Location.

Few ways this website could be used:

  • Find employers nearby who are sponsoring
  • Lookup open positions of past H1 sponsors
  • See what people nearby are getting paid for similar roles
  • Research pay at a prospective employer

All the information displayed here is publicly available LCA data from the US DOL.
